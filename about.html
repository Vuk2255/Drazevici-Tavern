Our Heritage

Drazevici Tavern stands as a testament to centuries-old tradition in the heart of Serbia. Our family has been crafting authentic rakija for generations, perfecting the art of distillation passed down through time. Starting from the fertile orchards of Drazevici, we carefully select the finest fruits at peak ripeness to create our distinctive spirits.

What began as a household tradition has blossomed into a celebration of Serbian culture and craftsmanship. Every bottle represents our unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and the preservation of ancestral knowledge.

Our Craft

The production of rakija is both an art and a science. We employ traditional distillation methods combined with modern quality standards to ensure every batch meets our exacting criteria. From orchard to bottle, we maintain complete control over our process.

Our master distillers bring decades of experience to each fermentation, ensuring consistent taste and exceptional smoothness. We believe that true quality cannot be rushed—each bottle is the result of patience, precision, and passion.

Our Vision

At Drazevici Tavern, we envision a world where traditional spirits are appreciated for their cultural significance and exceptional quality. We're dedicated to sharing the rich heritage of Serbian rakija with connoisseurs around the globe.

Beyond profit, our mission is to preserve centuries-old recipes and distillation techniques for future generations. We invite you to become part of our family, to experience the authentic taste of tradition, and to celebrate the art of fine spirits.
