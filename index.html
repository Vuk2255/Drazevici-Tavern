Our Story

Drazevici Tavern History

Our story begins in the well-tended orchards of our fertile Serbia, where we harvest fruits that have reached the peak of their ripeness. As our household has been renowned for almost a quarter of a century for the recipe of the queen of strong spirits—which captures the hearts and warms the palates of fine connoisseurs of homemade rakija, popularly known as the water of life—we felt the need to share our knowledge and craftsmanship with all lovers of tradition, enthusiasts of a fiery drop, and true epicures.
